WISH Leadership Experience

Women Involved in Software and Hardware is a support organization for women in computing majors and minors. A Cal Poly founded club in 2007, WISH strives to create an inclusive environment for women and allies who feel the constant struggles of being underrepresented in a predominately male profession.
